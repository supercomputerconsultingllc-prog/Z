# Campaign Approval Queue

Move approved drafts here before any sending workflow is considered.

## Status meanings
- `draft` - generated but not reviewed
- `approved` - approved for queueing
- `queued` - approved and ready for a later send step
- `sent` - actually sent
- `rejected` - do not send
- `suppressed` - blocked by suppression rules
