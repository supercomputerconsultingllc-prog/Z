# Supercomputer Consulting — 30-Day Outbound Plan

## Goal
Launch a simple, disciplined outbound motion that produces the first qualified conversations without turning the business into a spam machine.

---

## Month-one targets
- Build 60 target accounts
- Add 90–120 total contacts
- Send 45–60 personalized emails
- Send 30–40 LinkedIn connection requests
- Publish 4 short authority posts
- Book 2–4 discovery calls
- Generate at least 1 qualified opportunity

---

## Week 1 — Foundation and target list

### Objectives
- Tighten account list
- Prepare outreach assets
- Start first-touch outreach

### Tasks
- Finalize ICP segments and tiering
- Build first 25 target accounts
- Add 40–50 contacts
- Review / refine one-page overview
- Finalize email signature and short founder bio
- Send first 10 personalized emails
- Send first 8 LinkedIn connection requests
- Publish 1 credibility-building post

### End-of-week checkpoint
You should know:
- Which segment feels easiest to message
- Whether your positioning is too broad or sharp enough
- Whether replies indicate confusion or resonance

---

## Week 2 — Expand and follow up

### Objectives
- Continue first-touch outreach
- Start structured follow-up
- Identify early message winners

### Tasks
- Add 15 more accounts
- Add 20–30 more contacts
- Send 12–15 new personalized emails
- Follow up on week 1 sends
- Send 8–10 LinkedIn connection requests
- Ask 2 people for introductions
- Publish 1 post tied to common buyer risk

### End-of-week checkpoint
Review:
- Reply quality
- Open themes in objections
- Best-performing subject lines and opening angles

---

## Week 3 — Lean into signal

### Objectives
- Double down on the best segment or trigger
- Convert engagement into calls
- Tighten objection handling

### Tasks
- Add 10–15 accounts in the best-performing segment
- Send 12–15 new emails
- Follow up on all active conversations
- Send overview to engaged leads
- Run any discovery calls booked
- Publish 1 post or article excerpt on a high-stakes blind spot
- Update notes after every live conversation

### End-of-week checkpoint
Confirm:
- Which segment produces the most serious conversations
- Which CTA converts best
- Whether the pipeline has enough quality to justify more volume

---

## Week 4 — Convert and systematize

### Objectives
- Move serious leads forward
- Clean up the pipeline
- Build repeatable habits

### Tasks
- Send final close-the-loop follow-ups for stale leads
- Prepare proposals or scope outlines for qualified opportunities
- Move weak leads to nurture
- Publish 1 post summarizing a key lesson or market observation
- Review KPI dashboard
- Set next month’s focus segment and target list

### End-of-month review questions
- Which segment produced the strongest response quality?
- Which outreach message felt most natural and effective?
- How many replies turned into real conversations?
- What founder proof points or assets would most improve conversion next month?

---

## Suggested weekly activity floor
- 10–15 personalized emails
- 8–10 LinkedIn requests
- 10–15 follow-ups
- 1 referral ask or partner touch
- 1 content post

---

## Rules for month one
- Personalization beats volume
- Do not send undifferentiated blast campaigns
- Every active lead must have a next step
- Track objections in writing
- If a segment is weak after multiple touches, change the segment or message
- Focus on fit, not vanity metrics

---

## Simple daily plan

### Monday
Research accounts and build list

### Tuesday
Send first-touch emails

### Wednesday
Follow up and ask for introductions

### Thursday
Run calls and send recaps

### Friday
Post insight, review metrics, reset pipeline

---

## What success looks like after 30 days
By the end of the first month, success is not massive volume. Success is:
- a clean target list,
- a repeatable outreach rhythm,
- evidence of message-market fit,
- a small number of real conversations,
- and at least one qualified opportunity in motion.
