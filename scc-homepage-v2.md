## Differentiation section
**Not general consulting. Not commodity oversight.**

Supercomputer Consulting is built for a narrower category of engagement: situations where there is real downside to getting the call wrong.

That may mean:

- a facility acquisition with hidden capex exposure
- an expansion plan with weak operational assumptions
- an aging environment being pushed beyond its original design intent
- a mission-critical operation carrying more failure risk than leadership realizes
- an AI deployment moving faster than facility readiness

We help surface what matters, pressure-test the plan, and give leadership a cleaner basis for action.
