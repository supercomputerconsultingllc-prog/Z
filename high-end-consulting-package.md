## 9. Case Study Draft Templates

### Case Study 1 — Expansion planning
**Challenge:** A client was planning a facility expansion with significant capital implications and needed confidence that design assumptions matched operational reality.

**What we did:** Reviewed infrastructure plans, resilience assumptions, capacity constraints, and operational requirements. Identified key risks and areas where the proposed approach could create avoidable cost or long-term operational burden.

**Outcome:** Delivered a clearer decision framework, reduced uncertainty around infrastructure priorities, and helped the client move forward with greater confidence.

---

### Case Study 2 — Operational risk assessment
**Challenge:** A mission-critical environment needed an independent review of operational readiness, maintenance discipline, and hidden failure exposure.

**What we did:** Assessed operational practices, maintenance processes, staffing considerations, and facility resilience. Developed a prioritized remediation roadmap with immediate and longer-term recommendations.

**Outcome:** Helped leadership focus on the most significant risks first and created a practical path to improve reliability and reduce incident exposure.

---

### Case Study 3 — Modernization strategy
**Challenge:** An aging facility needed guidance on whether to modernize, reconfigure, or pursue a larger transition strategy.

**What we did:** Evaluated infrastructure condition, operational constraints, capacity limitations, and strategic business needs. Produced a practical modernization roadmap with decision points and risk tradeoffs.

**Outcome:** Gave stakeholders a structured basis for investment decisions and clarified where modernization would produce the greatest operational value.

---

### Case Study 4 — Due diligence support
**Challenge:** A client needed an experienced independent perspective before committing to a data center-related investment decision.

**What we did:** Reviewed facility conditions, operational maturity, resiliency posture, and likely remediation exposure. Summarized findings in an executive-friendly format with prioritized concerns.

**Outcome:** Improved visibility into risk, likely cost drivers, and operational considerations before capital was committed.
