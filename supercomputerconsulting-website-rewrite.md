## Service 1: Data Center Due Diligence
**Independent technical and operational review for acquisitions, investments, leases, and major facility decisions**

When capital is being committed, hidden infrastructure and operational issues can become expensive very quickly.

Supercomputer Consulting supports investors, acquirers, operators, and enterprise buyers with independent due diligence focused on facility risk, resiliency, operational maturity, and likely remediation exposure.

### Typical scope
- facility and infrastructure review
- power, cooling, redundancy, and resiliency assessment
- operational maturity and process review
- hidden capex or deferred maintenance exposure
- executive summary of key risks and priorities

### Best for
- acquisitions and investment reviews
- lease and capacity decisions
- portfolio assessments
- major pre-commitment technical review

---

## Service 2: Operational Risk & Resiliency Assessments
**Independent review of operations, maintenance, procedures, and failure exposure**

Many facilities appear sound until operational weaknesses, maintenance gaps, process failures, or hidden single points of failure are tested under pressure.

We assess the practices and conditions that affect real-world reliability and provide a prioritized roadmap for improvement.

### Typical scope
- review of operational readiness and procedures
- maintenance discipline and escalation review
- resilience and failure exposure analysis
- identification of single points of failure or weak operational controls
- prioritized remediation roadmap

### Best for
- facilities with uptime concerns
- leadership teams seeking an independent operational review
- post-incident reviews
- pre-audit or pre-customer readiness assessments

---

## Service 3: Owner’s Representative Advisory
**Owner-side guidance for new builds, expansions, retrofits, and complex facility initiatives**

Large facility projects often involve consultants, contractors, vendors, and internal stakeholders with competing assumptions and priorities.

Supercomputer Consulting acts as an experienced owner-side advisor to help ensure plans support actual operational needs, technical decisions are challenged appropriately, and avoidable mistakes are caught early.

### Typical scope
- review of design assumptions and decision points
- coordination support across project participants
- milestone review and technical oversight
- risk identification before issues become costly
- executive decision support during project delivery

### Best for
- new facilities
- facility expansions
- retrofits and infrastructure upgrades
- capital-intensive projects requiring experienced oversight
