# Supercomputer Consulting — One-Page Overview

## Independent advisory for high-stakes data center decisions

Supercomputer Consulting helps owners, operators, enterprises, and investors make better decisions across mission-critical facility strategy, resiliency, operations, modernization, due diligence, and growth.

**What clients want:** less uncertainty, fewer blind spots, better decision quality, and lower exposure to expensive mistakes.

---

## Who we help

- Data center owners and operators
- Enterprise infrastructure and facilities leaders
- Private equity and infrastructure investors
- Colocation, wholesale, and edge providers
- AI / HPC infrastructure teams

---

## Core advisory engagements

### Data Center Due Diligence
Independent review of facility risk, resiliency, operational maturity, and likely remediation exposure before major commitments are made.

### Operational Risk & Resiliency Assessments
Assessment of operations, maintenance practices, procedures, failure exposure, and improvement priorities.

### Owner’s Representative Advisory
Owner-side guidance for new builds, expansions, retrofits, and capital-intensive facility projects.

### Fractional Executive Advisory
Senior-level support for leadership teams managing complex infrastructure decisions without a full-time executive hire.

### AI / HPC Facility Readiness
Practical assessment of whether a facility can support higher-density compute and what upgrades or operational changes are required.

- A facility investment carries meaningful technical or operational risk
- Leadership needs an independent perspective, not a vendor sales pitch
- A project team needs experienced owner-side judgment during design or delivery
- Existing operations need a clearer view of resilience and hidden exposure
- AI / HPC growth is creating new power, cooling, and density constraints
