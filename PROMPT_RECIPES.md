# PROMPT_RECIPES.md

Use these as quick patterns when asking the assistant to do work.

## Email
- "Draft a reply to this email in a concise professional tone."
- "Create 10 personalized outbound email drafts for this list, but do not send them yet."
- "Review this email automation script and make it mobile-friendly."
- "Prepare a follow-up email sequence for warm leads, max 3 touches."

## Scheduling and reminders
- "Remind me tomorrow at 9 AM to call Alex about the proposal."
- "Set a recurring reminder every Monday at 8 AM to review pipeline."

## Research
- "Search the web for current GPU colocation pricing and summarize the findings."
- "Fetch this URL and give me the clean bullet-point summary."

## Files and documents
- "Turn these notes into a clean SOP."
- "Summarize the PDFs in workspace/inbox into markdown reports."
- "Find the latest screenshot and show it in chat."

## Automation and scripts
- "Inspect this script and harden the formatting logic."
- "Add a small improvement loop, but keep it bounded and auditable."
- "Spawn a sub-agent to investigate this bug and report back."

## Multi-step controlled outreach
- "Take this list, create personalized drafts, show me 3 samples, then wait for approval before sending anything."

## Capability introspection
- "What tools and skills do you currently have?"
- "What is missing from the current setup?"
- "Update the capability catalog after your changes."
