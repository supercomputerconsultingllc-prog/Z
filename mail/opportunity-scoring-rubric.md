# Opportunity Scoring Rubric

Use this rubric for business-relevant human emails.

## Score dimensions
Assign a light qualitative score for each:
- Fit: low / medium / high
- Urgency: low / medium / high
- Technical relevance: low / medium / high
- Likely value: low / medium / high
- Sender credibility: low / medium / high

## Buckets

### Weak lead
- vague curiosity
- poor fit
- low urgency
- little signal of real project momentum

### Possible lead
- plausible fit
- some relevant technical/business signal
- still needs clarification

### Strong lead
- clear need
- relevant project type
- credible sender or company
- meaningful urgency or complexity

### Urgent opportunity
- strong fit
- near-term timeline
- high stakes
- clear need for expert help

## Action mapping
- Weak lead -> brief helpful reply, keep warm, do not over-invest
- Possible lead -> qualify with 2-3 focused questions
- Strong lead -> qualify and suggest a concrete next step
- Urgent opportunity -> prioritize, respond quickly, suggest a call or rapid scoping step
